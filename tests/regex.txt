Old header style:

Find: (Windows CRLF)
\/(?:\*)+(?:.|\r|\n)*?EQdkp\r\n.*?Copyright (.*?)\r\n.*?\r\n.*?\r\n.*?\r\n \* Began\: ([A-Za-z]{3}) ([A-Za-z]{3})\w* (\d+) (\d+)\r\n.*?\r\n \* \$Id\: (.*?) (.*?) (\d+\-\d+\-\d+) (\d+\:\d+\:\d+)\w? (.*?) \$\r\n.*?\r\n.*?\*\/
or: (Any OS)
\/(?:\*)+(?:.|\r|\n)*?EQdkp(?:\r|\n)+.*?Copyright (.*?)(?:\r|\n)+.*?(?:\r|\n)+.*?(?:\r|\n)+.*?(?:\r|\n)+.*?\: ([A-Za-z]{3}) ([A-Za-z]{3})\w* (\d+) (\d+)(?:\r|\n)+.*?(?:\r|\n)+ \* \$Id\: (.*?) (.*?) (\d+\-\d+\-\d+) (\d+\:\d+\:\d+)\w? (.*?) \$(?:\r|\n)+.*?(?:\r|\n)+.*?\*\/

Matches:
1 - Copyright years

2 - Began Weekday
3 - Began Month
4 - Began Date
5 - Began Year

6 - Filename
7 - Revision
8 - Y-m-d
9 - H:i:s
10 - author

Replace With:
/**
 * Project:     EQdkp - Open Source Points System
 * License:     http://eqdkp.com/?p=license
 * -----------------------------------------------------------------------
 * File:        $6
 * Began:       $2 $3 $4 $5
 * Date:        $Date: $8 $9 +1000 (D, d m Y) $
 * -----------------------------------------------------------------------
 * @author      $Author: $10 $
 * @copyright   2002-2007 The EQdkp Project Team
 * @link        http://eqdkp.com/
 * @package     eqdkp
 * @version     $Rev: $7 $
 */
